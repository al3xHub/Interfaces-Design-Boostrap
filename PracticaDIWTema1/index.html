Presentation

GPT3 Directly in your Wordpress.
Create articles automatically thanks to GPT3 artificial intelligence.

Thanks to the use of OpenAI and its GPT3 language model we can create automatic websites generated with artificial intelligence. With our CH IA plugin you will achieve higher quality than traditional methods used on automatic websites, such as spintax or translation.

What can you achieve with this plugin?

Get keywords for titles

The only thing we will need is the list of titles you want for your articles. CH AI plugin will automate everything else.

Set up your 1st campaign

In 2 minutes you will have your 1st artificial intelligence campaign configured. Our management panel is simple and intuitive.

Add your OpenAI API

With OpenAI we will generate quality content with GPT3, a process that we will combine with ValueSerp and Google scraping to obtain headers and detect search intentions. The results will surprise you: long, well-themed articles with content in HTML format.

Professionalize your results

With the Premium version of CH AI Plugin you can configure your campaigns to the maximum, with personalized prompts, scheduling, insertion of videos and images, autocategorization of content, re-writing of headers and much more.

Caracteristics of Content Hero AI Plugin

Personalized Prompts

With the Premium version of the plugin you can customize the prompts with which the architecture and content of each article are generated: introduction, post content and conclusion. Personalized and quality prompts make the difference.

Multi-Language

CH AI plugin will help you create websites with AI in different languages: Spanish, English, French, German, Portuguese and Italian. If you want to create sites in another language, write to us and we will consider adding more languages that GPT-3 masters correctly

Images Generator

CH AI plugin también añade imágenes relacionadas en los artículos gracias al uso de 3 APIs distintas que son gratuitas. Podrás seleccionar la posición de las imágenes dentro del artículo y marcarlas como destacada.

Embedded videos

The best solution for your articles to correctly respond to the search intent is to add YouTube videos that provide information on the topic of the article. CH AI plugin incorporates this function in the Premium version.

HTML content and shortcodes

Thanks to the use of advanced prompts, the content generated with CH AI plugin will have HTML format: headings, lists, bold, paragraphs, etc., and you will also be able to use shortcodes and HTML within the content.

High quality velocity

Set up your campaign, select the publishing process and forget about it. The plugin works automatically 24 hours a day, and you will only have to go through the campaign to see how it is progressing.
